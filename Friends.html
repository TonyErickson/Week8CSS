Mario's Friends

Mario has lots of friends to help him with any issue or enemy that comes along!


Name/Information
Luigi : Luigi is Mario's brother. He tries to be brave but can be timid at times and is afraid of ghosts! However, he is willing to join his brother on any adventure!
Priness Peach : Peach is teh princess of the Mushroom Kingdom. She tries at many things such as adventuring, sport and kart racing.
Toad : Toad is one of the citizens of Mushroom Kingdom. He is loyal and cheerful. He is always happy to help whenever he is needed.
Rosalina : The mysterious Roasina came from outer space with her Luma, the lost star child. With her powerful wand, she is always calm in the face of danger.
Yoshi : Yoshi is laid back and likes to eat fruit. He helps Mario by using his long tongue to gobble up enemires. He can flutter his legs to jump really high to avoid danger.
Toadette : Todette lives in the Mushroom Kingdom. She is brave and always ready for an adventure.
Donkey Kong : Donkey Kong is known for his strength. Bananas are Donkey Kongs favorite food.
